Offering fast, friendly, and complete boat repair and maintenance services

Northwest Marine Painting provides full service marine finish painting, aesthetics, and repairs in the Seattle and Tacoma areas. We repair and refinish any style and type of boat or watercraft vessel whether old or new using the best products in the marine industry. We offer either on-site services or you can bring your parts in to us. Our services include the following:
Painting, Gel Coat, Varnish, Fairing, Fiberglass Repair, Sandblasting

Inflatable boat repair

  • We can repair you inflatable boat, including air leaks, handle valve replacement, and tow rings

    We can also install weaver davit systems. We clean inflatable boats to their original shine.

  • We work on these fine brands:

    AB, Achilles, Avon, Caribe, Nautica, Novurania, Zodiac

  • Licensed, bonded and insured
