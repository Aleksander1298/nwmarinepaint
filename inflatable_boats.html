Inflatable boat painting and repair services

We Repair Inflatable Boats

We can repair you inflatable boat. We fix air leaks, replace handle valves, and tow rings

We install Weaver Davit Systems

Weaver snap davits are installed on hard-bottomed or inflatable dinghies, to attach to yachts. We install davit systems to fit your needs

All Types of Inflatables

We repair and refinish any style or type of inflatable whether new or old. Bring us your parts or we can come to you. Some brands we work on are: AB, Achilles, Avon, Caribe, Nautica, Novurania, and Zodiac

Refinishing your Inflatable

Is your boat looking old and dull? We can clean your boat to look like new again.
